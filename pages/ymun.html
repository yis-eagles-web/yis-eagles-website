Get Ready for the YMUN Experience

During the MUN conferences, high school students get to roleplay as delegates from other counties. They work together to solve and write resolutions for real world problems like climate change, human right issues and global health issues. Students can discuss important topics, negotiate with other delegates, and create solutions in their own committees. MUN conferences are not just about speaking well and debating but also about listening, collaborating, and thinking critically. The MUN conferences provide students with an understanding of the challenges our world faces and how great teamwork leads to great solutions. What makes YMUN special? YMUN isn't just about committee sessions. There is so much more to explore during the conferences. You’ll hear from many people who are experts in different ways that will share their knowledge and inspire you to think and be better at debating. These YMUN conferences will also help you build skills like public speaking, research, critical thinking and leadership. It also gives you a chance to meet students from many different schools in Yangon and gives you a broader perspective that you can't find in a classroom. At the end of the conferences, you can also receive awards such as the best delegate, most diploaticand so on! Now, why should you participate in YMUN? The YMUN conferences are not just about focusing on winning awards but also focus on growing as a person.

You’ll leave the conference with better communication skills, more confidence and a deeper understanding of issues around the world. It is also a great way to make friends who might have the same interests as you. For many students, YMUN is a where their character developed in high school. It helps them see how they can make a difference through leadership, teamwork, or creative problem-solving. If you have ever thought of ways you can contribute to global challenges or are interested in issues around the world, YMUN is a great way to start. To sum this all up, YMUN is more than just a conference, it's a chance to grow, learn, debate, and be inspired. Whether you’re solving problems in your committee, making frenemies, or learning from the Chairs (student leaders of a committee that make sure that the committee's work is completed smoothly and successfully), you’ll walk away with new skill and amazing memories.
